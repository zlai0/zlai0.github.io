About

I am a researcher in Visual Geometry Group at Oxford, advised by Prof. Andrew Zisserman. Before that, I worked with Prof. Kilian Q. Weinberger and Dr. Huang Gao during a nice summer in Cornell University. I have also previously worked in CPS Group at Oxford with Prof. Niki Trigoni and Dr. Andrew Markham. My research interests are Computer Vision and Machine Learning.
